Why Techhub?

Techhub is basically designed to solve the problem of communication gap between the collegemates. Students in colleges basically live in friend circle and outside their friend circle the hardly know about the skills of other individuals. techhub is basically designed to bridge that communication gap between the individuals and will help individuals to know the skills of others.

This site is basically designed to serve the following purposes:

Those who want to seek guidance from others who are expert in a particular technology like machine learning , web development,etc .

Those who are seeking for project patners for hackathons and coding competitions like ACM-ICPC regionals.
